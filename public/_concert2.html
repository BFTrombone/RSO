Our December concert weekend will feature beloved holiday favorites, as well as traditional classical pieces that are associated with the holidays or spark the essence of the holiday season. Gather with your loved ones and join us for a concert filled with joy and delight!

Our December concert weekend will feature beloved holiday favorites, as well as traditional classical pieces that are associated with the holidays or spark the essence of the holiday season. Joining us on this concert will be Seattle Opera's Ryan Bede to lead the audience through a carol sing along, and to take the stage as our baritone soloist on some of my favorite Christmas tunes.

Gather with your loved ones and join us for a concert filled with joy and delight!
