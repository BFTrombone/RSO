Auditions

Tuesday evening, August 30, auditions will be held for open positions and for substitutes, extras and utility players. For more information and to request an audition time, please email the orchestra at audition@rainiersymphony.org.

If you are interested in playing with the orchestra but unavailable to audition on August 30th, you may be able to schedule an audition at a later date.

The orchestra uses any of several methods to determine whether an invitation to join the ensemble as a regular member will be extended. These methods can include traditional audition format before a committee and also sitting in with the orchestra at one or more rehearsals. In most cases, musicians will be expected to play prepared music of their choosing that demonstrates their musical abilities and familiarity with and command of orchestral repertoire. We recommend approximately 10 minutes of prepared material, which should include part of a major concerto and orchestral excerpts.

Rainier Symphony has current openings for the following positions:

  • Principal Trumpet
  • Section Trumpet
  • Section Percussion
  • Section Violin

In addition to the open positions, the August 30th auditions are for all musicians (any instrument) wishing to be included on the orchestra's list of substitutes, extras and utility instrument players. Specific audition times will be scheduled as soon as possible.

Please send audition inquiries to audition@rainiersymphony.org.
