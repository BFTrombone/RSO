February Concerts

If you purchased tickets to the snow-cancelled February concerts:

  • If you purchased single concert tickets on-line, you will automatically receive a refund from Brown Paper Tickets. You do not need to take any action to receive your refund.
  • If you purchased a season ticket package (whether on-line or in-person), you have three options:
    1. Discard the tickets, and the price paid becomes a donation to Rainier Symphony. A tax receipt can be provided upon request; email "questions@rainiersymphony.org" if so.
    2. Exchange the tickets for a concert in April or June 2019. You can do this at the Box Office on the day of the concert, or mail them to Rainier Symphony with a note specifying the concert date (4/6, 4/7, 6/1, 6/2).
    3. Request a refund of one fifth of the season ticket price. To do this, mail the ticket(s) to Rainier Symphony and enclose a note with your name and address. We will mail a check with your refund within 2 weeks of receipt.
  • If purchased through an orchestra member:
    1. Discard the tickets, and the price paid becomes a donation to Rainier Symphony. A tax receipt can be provided upon request; email "questions@rainiersymphony.org" if so.
    2. Exchange the tickets for a concert in April or June 2019. You can do this through your orchestra member contact, at the Box Office on the day of the concert, or mail them to Rainier Symphony with a note specifying the concert date (4/6, 4/7, 6/1, 6/2).
    3. Request a refund. You can do this by giving the tickets to your orchestra member contact (s/he would bring them to a future rehearsal), or mail the ticket(s) to Rainier Symphony and enclose a note with your name and address. Either way, we will mail a check with your refund within 2 weeks of receipt.
  • Mailing address:
    Rainier Symphony, PO Box 58182, Seattle WA 98138
  • When sending mail, please be sure to include your name, address, and a contact phone or email in case we have questions.
