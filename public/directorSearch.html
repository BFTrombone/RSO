Music Director Search

Now in its 34th season, Rainier Symphony continues to build on its legacy of excellence as we move forward with a commitment to many more seasons of great music making.

This season we are pleased to present five excellent guest conductors. Beginning with the 2015-16 season, the orchestra will rehearse and perform with the finalists for the Music Director position.

We are currently accepting applications for the position of Music Director, which combines the roles of Conductor and Artistic Advisor. For information about the role and the application process, please see the Job Description and Applicant Information documents.

APPLICATION DEADLINE: Sunday, February 1, 2015

Timeline:

  • November 15, 2014: Begin accepting applications
  • February 1, 2015: Application deadline
  • November 2014 - April 2015: Review applications, watch videos, interview candidates, and talk with references
  • May 2015: Announce finalists
  • June 2015: Align finalists to the 2015-16 concerts and select repertoire
  • September 2015: Begin rehearsing with the first finalist
  • May/June 2016: Announce the new Music Director

Questions? Please email mdsearch@rainiersymphony.org
