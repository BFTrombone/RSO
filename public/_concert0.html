2015 - 2016 Season

Dates for concerts in the 2015-16 season will be announced soon. Check back later in June for the concert schedule and information on the five finalists for the position of Music Director.

Interested in becoming an orchestra member? We currently have openings for violin, double bass, horn, trumpet, and percussion, as well as substitutes for most sections. We will hold auditions in late summer or early fall. Rainier Symphony is a non-paid volunteer orchestra, and rehearses on Tuesday nights. If interested, please send an email to audition@rainiersymphony.org.
