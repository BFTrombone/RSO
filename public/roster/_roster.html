Rainier Symphony is a non-paid volunteer orchestra, and rehearses Tuesday evenings from 7:15-9:45 pm on stage at Foster Performing Arts Center in Tukwila. In addition to Tuesday rehearsals, we have Friday dress rehearsals. Rehearsals are held most weeks from September through May, with a break in December.
