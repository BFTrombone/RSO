Todd Frazier, French Horn

Return to Roster

Todd Frazier has served as 3rd horn with Rainier Symphony for 14 years. He also is a member of the Gateway Concert Band, Black Diamond Brass (quintet), and Webb of Life (praise group at his church). He can be heard on occasion in the horn sections of Northwest Mahler Festival Orchestra, Octava Chamber Orchestra, Seattle Philharmonic, and Orchestra Seattle as well as any other area orchestra, church, concert band, or small ensemble that needs a horn.

Before moving to the Seattle area in 2001, Todd has been principal horn with the Topeka Symphony, and before that 2nd horn with the Flagstaff Symphony. He earned a degree in music education from Northern Arizona University in 1988 and taught music for several years. In 2011, Todd completed his Masters in Education with an additional certification in special education. He currently teaches special needs students at CHILD Institute in Renton.

In his spare time, Todd reads science fiction, dabbles in carpentry, and attempts to play golf.
