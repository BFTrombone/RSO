Jeff Lund


A native of the Pacific Northwest, Jeffery N. Lund is a celebrated conductor, educator, and percussionist based in the Puget Sound area. He has conducted the West Seattle Symphony, the Central Washington University Orchestra, the CWU Chamber Orchestra and Contemporary Chamber Ensemble, the Tacoma Community College Orchestra, the Central Michigan Symphony Orchestra, and is currently Academy Orchestra Conductor with Student Orchestras of Greater Olympia.

As a guest conductor, Jeffery has been featured at the Northwest Percussion Festival, Puget Brass, the Mid-Michigan Composers Symposium, and the CD release concerts for soloist Lenny Price, and singer-songwriter Kris Orlowski. Each summer, Jeffery holds residency at the Evergreen Music Festival, where he conducts the chamber orchestra and is the Festival’s percussion teaching artist. Additional engagements have given him the opportunity to conduct the Tacoma Youth Symphony, the Tacoma Young Artists Orchestra, the Everett Youth Symphony, the Kittitas Valley Youth Orchestra, and the Conservatory Orchestra of Student Orchestras of Greater Olympia.

Jeffery has previously held the positions of Music Director with the Capital Youth Symphony of Olympia, Administrative Director with the Kittitas Valley Youth Orchestra, and that of percussion teaching artist and Percussion Ensemble Director with the Tacoma Youth Symphony Association. Jeffery continually strives for excellence in music education through dynamic programming and engagement opportunities for students and their communities.

In addition to his conducting activities Jeffery is the Affiliate Artist in Percussion at the University of Puget Sound. He has performed as soloist with numerous orchestras, wind ensembles, and chamber groups across the United States. Recognized as a passionate and engaging educator, Jeffery has been invited to lecture at numerous percussion festivals and competitions around the country, most recently as a guest soloist and lecturer for the Northwest Mallet Festival. He stays active as an orchestral musician. Locally, he may be seen playing with the Yakima Symphony Orchestra, the Tacoma Symphony Orchestra, the Federal Way Symphony, and the Seattle Choral Company. Frequent guest appearances have allowed him to collaborate with composers such as Ney Rosauro, Dave Hollinden, Daniel Montoya Jr., Zhou Long, Julie Spencer, Nicolas Papador, Jeff Brooks, and Cindy McTee.

Jeffery holds a Master of Music degree in orchestral conducting from Central Washington University and a Master of Music degree in percussion performance from Central Michigan University. He is a Liberace scholar, a Hubbard Scholarship recipient, and is an endorsed artist with Marimba One.

Jeffery resides in Tacoma where he teaches percussion students at his home studio and studies violin. He enjoys woodworking, landscaping, and hiking.
