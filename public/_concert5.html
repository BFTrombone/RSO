Our 2019-2020 season ends with an energetic and thoughtful collage of beloved classical favorites. Our Musical Mysticism pops concert will be comprised of very familiar orchestral works that you’ll recognize from movies, television, animations, and of course, the radio.

On this program, we’ll hear 7 different pieces that all connect to the nature of mystic scenario. Whether we’re dashing down a mountain, stumbling upon a faun in the woods, watching a young magician get in over his head, or travelling to the fantasy world of William Shakespeare, you and your loved ones will be catapulted into a mystic depiction of enchanting and delightful scenes, with enough diversity to ensure there’s something for everyone.

We hope to see you as we close out our 2019-2020 season with an eclectic and enjoyable blend of excellent light-classics.
