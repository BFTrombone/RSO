A Salute to Broadway

Saturday, June 1 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, June 2 - 3:00 pm

This year's pops concert will feature Broadway singer Katie Travis as she takes you on a tour of some of her favorite musicals and Broadway songs. She'll also be joining the orchestra on Samuel Barber's Knoxville Summer: 1915. Closing out our entire 2018-19 season will be Bernstein's Symphonic Dances from West Side Story, an orchestral suite comprised of themes and scenes from his beloved musical of the same name.
