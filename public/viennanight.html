New Year's Eve Night in Vienna 2007!


Celebrate New Year's Eve in spectacular fashion as Rainier Symphony brings Vienna to the Doubletree Guest Suites. Highlights of the evening include a three-course gourmet meal and dancing accompanied by the Rainier Symphony Strauss Orchestra and the Chris Johansen Jazz Quartet. A special performance by The Strauss Dancers will inspire you to dance your way into the New Year at the best Viennese Ball this side of the Danube!
