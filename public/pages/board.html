Rainier Symphony Board of Trustees / Staff
The Rainier Symphony is a non-profit corporation - 501(c)3 is managed by key members of the community dedicated to bringing affordable, great classical music to South King County. The Board of Trustees along with a small but enthusiastic staff work to provide a place for talented volunteer musicians from all walks of life to come together and perform as the Rainier Symphony.
