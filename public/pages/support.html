The Rainier Symphony is your symphony.  We are your friends, neighbors, and fellow citizens of South King County and beyond.

Funding for the arts is becoming scarce in these economic times - we want to thank all of the corporate and individual sponsors of the Rainier Symphony and ask you to think of creative ways to continue to support the Rainier Symphony.

Besides providing a place for talented volunteer musicians to perform, we actively support music programs in local schools.  Music is an important part of our lives and we ask for your support in bringing it to our children.
