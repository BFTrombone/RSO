Celestial Voices

Saturday, April 6 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, April 7 - 3:00 pm

Our final Classics Concert of the season will feature a collaboration with a local women's choir on Claude Debussy's Nocturnes and Gustav Holst's The Planets. This ethereal music captures the spirit of children's dreams, and leaves this world on an exploration of ethereal colors, textures, images and voices.
