Celestial Voices

Saturday, April 6 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, April 7 - 3:00 pm

Foster Performing Arts Center
4242 South 144th Street, Tukwila

We're very pleased to be able to reschedule one of the selections from our snow-cancelled February concerts: our principal oboist Michael Melnick will step out of the wind section to solo on Vaughan-Williams' pastoral Oboe Concerto in A minor. We will also perform Claude Debussy's Nocturnes and Gustav Holst's most famous work, The Planets. This music leaves this world on an exploration of ethereal colors, textures, images and voices.

Debussy Nocturnes
Vaughan-Williams Concerto in A minor for Oboe and Strings
Michael Melnick, Oboe
Holst The Planets
