Poems: Prophetic and Pastoral

Saturday, February 9 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, February 10 - 3:00 pm

Our 2nd Classics Concert this season will feature music that aligns itself with literary and visual art forms. Consisting of 3 tone poems, the bulk of this concert is meant to depict pastoral scenes from the countryside, commentary on political revolutions, and an existential view of the journey from birth to death. Also on this program our principal oboist, Michael Melnick, will step out of the winds section to solo on Vaughan-Williams' pastoral Oboe Concerto in A minor.

Smetana The Moldau
Vaughan-Williams Concerto in A minor for Oboe and Strings
Michael Melnick, Oboe
Shostakovich October, Op. 131
Liszt Les Préludes
