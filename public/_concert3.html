A Relentless Determination

Saturday, February 22 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, February 23 - 3:00 pm

Foster Performing Arts Center
4242 South 144th Street, Tukwila

Join us for a concert experience built on the idea of persistence, determination, and a relentless desire to continue moving forward, despite all obstacles. On this program, we’ll feature music by Verdi, Lalo, and Beethoven.

We are thrilled to feature our principal cellist, Tom Walworth, on the Lalo Cello Concerto in February! Tom is a long-standing member of the orchestra, and a splendid musician. One of the perks of an orchestra like Rainier Symphony is our ability to feature our own musicians as soloists, and still present concerts of the highest art.

Bookending the concert, the concert will open with Verdi’s Overture to Nabucco, an opera set shortly before an impending war between the Israelites and a Babylonian army. After intermission, the orchestra will perform Beethoven’s most famous work: Symphony no. 5 in C Minor. This piece is performed dozens of times annually around the globe and hasn’t been performed by Rainier Symphony in over 25 years.

Join us for a concert experience built on the idea of persistence, determination, and a relentless desire to continue moving forward, despite all obstacles.

Verdi Overture to Nabucco
Lalo Cello Concerto
   Tom Walworth, Cello
Beethoven Symphony No. 5 in C minor, Op. 67
