A Relentless Determination

Saturday, February 22 - 7:30 pm
Sunday, February 23 - 3:00 pm

Foster Performing Arts Center
4242 South 144th Street, Tukwila

Join us for a concert experience built on the idea of persistence, determination, and a relentless desire to continue moving forward, despite all obstacles. On this program, we’ll feature music by Verdi, Lalo, and Beethoven.

We are thrilled to feature our principal cellist, Tom Walworth, on the Lalo Cello Concerto in February! Tom is a long-standing member of the orchestraas well as a splendid musician. One of the unique qualities of an orchestra like Rainier Symphony amongst other community orchestras, is our ability to feature our own musicians as soloists, and present concerts of the highest artistic quality.

Bookending the Tom's concerto, the concert will open with Verdi’s Overture to Nabucco, an opera set shortly before an impending war between the Israelites and a Babylonian army. After intermission, the orchestra will perform Beethoven’s most famous work: Symphony no. 5 in C Minor. This piece is one of the most popular pieces in the orchestral repertoire, and is performed dozens of times each year all over the world.

Join us for a concert experience built on the idea of persistence, determination, and a relentless desire to continue moving forward, despite all obstacles.

Verdi Overture to Nabucco
Lalo Cello Concerto
   Tom Walworth, Cello
Beethoven Symphony No. 5 in C minor, Op. 67
