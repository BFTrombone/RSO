Concert Recordings

Recordings of the Rainier Symphony are strictly for use by Rainier Symphony members. All others are prohibited from accessing this website.

Note: Concert recordings are kept on Google Drive. You can play them in your browser or you can download the file. To download these, click on the link, then click on the down arrow on the upper right corner of the new page.

  1. Click on the link for the desired performance.
  2. If you are signed into a Google account, click on the down arrow at the top right of the page.
  3. If you are not signed into a Google account, you'll get a Whoops! message with a download button. Go ahead and click on the download button.
  4. If you get a page that says Google Drive can't scan the file for viruses, go ahead and click Download Anyway.
  5. Download the file and open it with your favorite ZIP file utility.
