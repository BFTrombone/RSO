Concert Recordings

Recordings of the Rainier Symphony are strictly for use by Rainier Symphony members. All others are prohibited from accessing this website.

Recordings are in uncompressed .WAV format and saved inside a ZIP file. To download a concert, click the ZIP file for the desired concert. Downloads may take ten minutes or more depending on your connection. Once the ZIP file has finished downloading to your computer, use a ZIP file utility to extract the WAV files for the concert. If you need to install a ZIP utility on your computer, you can use ninite.com. Select PeaZip and follow the instructions to install.

NOTE: Video files are much larger than the audio files e.g. the 12/12/2014 Holiday Concert Video is over 2 GB. Be aware of this when downloading these.
