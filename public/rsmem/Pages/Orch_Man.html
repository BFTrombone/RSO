Absences

One excused absence is allowed for a concert having four or fewer rehearsals. 

Two excused absences for concerts having more than four rehearsals.   If a player is not required to attend all rehearsals because they do not play in every piece, the number of excused absences allowed will be based upon the number of rehearsals they are required to attend.

An excused absence is one in which the player notifies both the section leader and Orchestra Manager AS SOON AS THE DATE OF ABSENCE BECOMES KNOWN.

For woodwinds, brass, and percussion, the player must make every attempt to find an appropriately qualified substitute.  When possible, the section leader and personnel manager will assist in finding a sub.  Also, woodwind, brass, and percussion players must make arrangements to deliver music to rehearsal.  Any failure will result in the absence being considered unexcused.
