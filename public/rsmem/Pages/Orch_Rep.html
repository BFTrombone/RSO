We are your voice and represent you, the Symphony members on the Rainier Symphony Board of Trustees. 

In addition, we chair the Orchestra Committee, which works to make the Rainier Symphony an attractive place for talented musicians, such as yourself.
 

Questions?  Concerns?  Call us.
Please call us or send us an email if there are topics that you would like to discuss or want brought up to the Board of Trustees on how things are working.  We will keep any discussions confidential if that is what you want. You may email the entire Orchestra Committee at orch_comm@rainiersymphony.org.

We also want you to know that you are invited to any Rainier Symphony Board meeting.  If you are interested in attending, please give either of us a call and we'll let you know when the next meeting is scheduled.


Orchestra Committee

The primary functions of the Orchestra Committee are:

  • To recommend policy as it relates to Rainier Symphony players
  • To hear petitions and grievances of players
  • To mediate in personnel matters among and between players
  • To serve as a conduit of communication between players and the Music Director/conductor
  • To work in cooperation with the personnel manager
  • To participate in the health, growth, and direction of the organization

You can contact the Orchestra Representatives or any of the members of the Orchestra Committee to discuss any ideas or comments that you have.
