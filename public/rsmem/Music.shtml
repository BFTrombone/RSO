Note from our Librarian - Dianne Lange Jones

Music is one of our largest expenses. We depend on you to help us manage our costs:

Music check-out:

Each player is assigned a numbered folder with corresponding numbered parts. At the end of each concert, each player must return all parts with the folder, including all copied parts. If for some reason a player can't return the music himself/herself, please notify Diane who will be returning the folder.

Rental Music:

If a rented part is lost, the orchestra is responsible for payment (usually a large amount) due to the publisher, so please be very careful of any rental music. Remember to erase any changes on the individual part, and never use pen.
