Auditions

Rainier Symphony rehearses on Tuesday evenings from 7:15-9:45 pm at Foster Performing Arts Center in Tukwila. In addition to Tuesday rehearsals we have Friday evening dress rehearsals. This season includes pairs of concerts each in October, February, April and May in Tukwila in addition to a holiday concert on a Friday in December. For a detailed description of the orchestra please visit: rainiersymphony.org. Repertoire will be listed under the calendar section.

For auditions, we ask that musicians prepare two contrasting styles of music to play (no more than 6 minutes total) and then be prepared to sightread.

Please send audition inquiries to audition@rainiersymphony.org.
