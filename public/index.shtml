Rainier Symphony Friends and Families

Welcome back to another exciting season of music making with the Rainier Symphony! Our 2019-2020 concert calendar is filled with truly splendid music, and will feature amazing musicians as soloists throughout the year! Our own principal cellist, Tom Walworth, will perform the Lalo Cello Concerto, we’ll have Dr. Erik Steighner join us on the Higdon Concerto for Soprano Saxophone, and baritone Ryan Bede will join us to sing on our holiday program.

This season you can anticipate hearing music that is familiar and beloved the world-over, such as Beethoven’s Symphony No. 5 in C Minor, Smetana’s The Moldau, Elgar’s Enigma Variations, and Debussy’s Prelude to the Afternoon of a Faun. You’ll also hear some less familiar compositions that you are sure to enjoy, such as Sibelius’ Symphony No. 7, Shostakovich’s October, Op. 131, and the Higdon Concerto for Soprano Saxophone. Each concert has been thoughtfully programmed to provide a truly enjoyable musical experience that has an organic arch from piece to piece.
