Announcing Our New Music Director!

After a wonderful season filled with great music as we worked under the batons of five talented music director candidates, we are pleased to announce that Jeffery Lund has accepted the position of Rainier Symphony Music Director.

The Rainier Symphony has had only six music directors in its 35-year history, which reflects the community and family spirit of the organization. Choosing a new music director is a big decision. We're all looking forward to working with Jeff for many seasons to come. Jeff, welcome to the Rainier Symphony family!

More about Jeffery N. Lund.

A Note from the Director

Greetings! It is truly my pleasure to be given the opportunity to work with the Rainier Symphony as Conductor and Music Director. I am thrilled at the prospect of the wonderful music we will make together. I believe we have an exciting year ahead of us and that this season's concerts will not only offer enjoyable experiences, but will also provide inspirational and musically gratifying connections among all orchestra members and the listening community, as well.

Thank you to the Rainier Symphony Board of Directors for putting your trust in me and welcoming me to lead the Rainier Symphony.

- Jeffery N. Lund
