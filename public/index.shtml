2017 - 2018 Season

Greetings friends of the Rainier Symphony! After a short summer off, we're excited to return for another season of wonderful programs you will definitely enjoy. This season will bring you compositions from many musical generations: young and old. Each concert is designed to introduce pieces and composers that may be unfamiliar to you. Fear not, these "new" pieces will be accompanied by some of the most popular symphonic works in the orchestral repertoire. Join us to hear your most beloved classical themes, and you may leave each concert with a new favorite.

Also, as a gesture of gratitude to our ardent patrons, we have reduced the cost of season tickets in order to increase the discount to subscribers relative to the price of individually purchased tickets. If you're already a season ticket holder, please enjoy this lower ticket price, and thank you for your support. If you're not yet a season ticket holder, it's not too late! You can find information on our entire concert season on the website, as well as information on purchasing season tickets. We know there are lots of ways to spend your weekends, and we're grateful you spend our concert weekends with us!
